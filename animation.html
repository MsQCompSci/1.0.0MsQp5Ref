About Ms. Quinones

Layla Quinones teaches Computer Science & STEM at MS/HS 223 in the Bronx. She has a BS and MA from NYU in Physics & Education, and is focused on spreading CS to communities throughout NYC. She is passionate about integrating CS across the curriculum and identifying entry points for CS in other subjects.
